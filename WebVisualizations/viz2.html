How do city latitude and humdity affect the aurora borealis?

This is a scatterplot analyzing latitude and humidity. There isn't much of a correlation between latitude and humidity. The optimal latitude for viewing the aurora borealis is 60 degrees or less. This graph tells us that cities north of 60 degrees latitude are similar in humidity to cities south of that, so humidity is not a factor we should worry about when planning a trip to see the aurora borealis.
