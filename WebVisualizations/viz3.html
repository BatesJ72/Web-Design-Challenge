How do city latitude and cloudiness affect the aurora borealis?

This is a scatterplot analyzing latitude and cloudiness. Obviously, we don't want any clouds on our trip! There isn't any correlation between latitude and cloudiness - the amount of cloudiness in cities south of 60 degress latitude is the same as it is in the cities north of that. There is a drop in observations north of 75 degrees, but that is most likely due to a lack of observations more than a lack of cloudiness. We'll need to look more at time of year and other factors to determine cloudiness, rather than just latitude.
